# **NextLabs Rights Management Service**

- Module name: nxrmserv.exe
- Type: Windows Service

NextLabs Rights Management Service is the essential part of rights Management Client (for Windows) which is responsible for:

- Modules management
- Data management
- Communication
- Diagnostic
- Statistic
